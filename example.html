HTML Magnifier Example

How to use: Click and drag (or touch and drag on mobile) to activate the magnifier. The magnifier will appear in the top-left corner showing a 2x zoomed view of the content under your cursor.

Sample Content

This is a simple example demonstrating the HTML Magnifier library.

Try dragging your mouse (or finger on mobile) across this page to see the magnifier in action!

Features:

  • Works with any HTML content
  • Supports dynamic canvas and SVG elements
  • Mouse and touch support
  • Non-intrusive - doesn't interfere with normal interactions
  • Resource efficient - only captures while dragging
